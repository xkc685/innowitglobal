About InnoWit

A Global Leader in Energy Storage Solutions

Since 2010, InnoWit has been at the forefront of energy storage innovation, specializing in LiFePO4 batteries, energy storage systems, EV chargers, and solar inverters. We deliver turnkey systems — from product innovation to hybrid and off-grid installations.

With a 10,000m² production facility and products certified to CE, UL 1973, IEC 62619, and UN38.3 standards, we serve customers in over 100 countries across residential, commercial, and utility-scale applications.
