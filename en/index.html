About InnoWit

Among the Top Energy Storage Manufacturers

InnoWit is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in the R&D, manufacturing, and sales of LiFePO4 energy storage batteries. With over 15 years of industry experience, a 50,000m² automated production facility, and products certified to CE, UL 1973, IEC 62619, and UN38.3 standards, we serve customers in more than 30 countries worldwide.

Our product portfolio spans residential wall-mounted batteries, commercial storage cabinets, industrial containerized systems, and off-grid solutions — providing reliable, safe, and efficient energy storage for every scenario.
