About InnoWit

National High-Tech Enterprise · Professional LiFePO4 Energy Storage Battery Manufacturer

Home / About

Among the Top 10 LiFePO4 Energy Storage Manufacturers

The company was founded in 2010 as an OEM manufacturer for energy storage products. In 2015, we introduced fully automated production lines, established a comprehensive testing laboratory, and obtained ISO 9001 certification. By 2018, our products achieved CE, UL 1973, and IEC 62619 international certifications, expanding sales to 20+ countries.

Headquartered in a 50,000m² industrial park, our factory features automated assembly lines, laser welding stations, and a central testing laboratory capable of performing over 50 types of safety and performance tests. Annual production capacity exceeds 2GWh.
